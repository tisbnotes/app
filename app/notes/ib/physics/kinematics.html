Kinematics

Motion

Definitions

  • Distance travelled: the length of the path followed by an object.
  • Speed: the rate of change of distance.
  • Displacement: the change in position of the object.
  • Velocity: the rate of change of displacement.
    • Instantaneous velocity: the speed at any given moment when an object is moving.
  • Acceleration: the rate of change of velocity.
    • Acceleration of free fall (on earth): 9.8ms−19.8 \text{ms}^{-1}9.8ms−1
