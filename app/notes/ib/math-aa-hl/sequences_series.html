Superannuation

This is when compound interest is applied over a certain number of periods, while there is money being deposited into the account every period too.
Sn=a(1+r100)+a(1+r100)2+⋯+a(1+r100)n S_n = a \left( 1 + \frac{r}{100} \right) + a \left( 1 + \frac{r}{100} \right)^2 + \dots + a \left( 1 + \frac{r}{100} \right) ^n Sn​=a(1+100r​)+a(1+100r​)2+⋯+a(1+100r​)n
