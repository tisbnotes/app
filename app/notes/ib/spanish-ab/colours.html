Spanish Colours

  • The colours ending with ‘o’ change their ending to -a depending on the gender of the noun they’re describing.
    • Una mochila amarilla
    • Un libro amarillo
  • The others do not change their gender.
    • Una mesa verde
    • Un libro verde
  • If the nouns are plural, then the colour is plural.
    • Unos bolígrafos azules.
