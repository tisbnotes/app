Improving Vocabulary

Le Comte de Monte-Cristo
The above is an e-book of ‘Le Comte de Monte-Cristo’ by Alexandre Dumas. This will be very useful to improve vocabulary. Although this is around or slightly above HL level, the different phrases and expressions will be invaluable to your vocabulary, I recommend reading it. The story itself is also quite interesting.

Translation/Spellchecking

Reverso.net
The above is a preferred alternative to Google Translate, in fact, it is greatly recommended as it is very authentic and colloquial with its translations and expressions. Whenever you need to spellcheck or translate something, always consider using this site over Google Translate.
