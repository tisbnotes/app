1. Nice to meet you. What’s your name?

老师 可以进来吗？
Kěyǐ jìnlái ma?
May I come in?
林娜 请进! 杨老师,您好. 这是我朋友, 他是记者.
Qǐng jìn! Yáng lǎoshī, nín hǎo. Zhè shì wǒ péngyǒu, tā shì jìzhě.
Please come in! Teacher Yang, hello. This is my friend, he is a reporter.
老师 请问, 您贵姓？
Qǐngwèn, nín guìxìng?
Excuse me, what is your name?
陆雨平 我姓陆, 叫陆雨平.
Wǒ xìng Lù, jiào Lù Yǔpíng.
My surname is Lu, called Lu Yuping.
老师 你好, 陆先生, 认识你很高兴.
Nǐ hǎo, Lù xiānshēng, rènshì nǐ hěn gāoxìng.
Hello, Mr. Lu, I am very happy to meet you.
陆雨平 杨老师, 认识您, 我也很高兴.
Yáng lǎoshī, rènshì nín, wǒ yě hěn gāoxìng.
Teacher Yang, I am also very happy to meet you.

2. Where are you from?

林娜 我是语言学院的学生. 我姓林, 叫林娜. 我是英国人. 你姓什么?
Wǒ shì Yǔyán Xuéyuàn de xuéshēng. Wǒ xìng Lín, jiào Lín Nà. Wǒ shì yīngguó rén. Nǐ xìng shénme?
I am a student of Language Institution. My name is Lin, called Lin Na. I’m British. What is your last name?
马大为 我姓马, 叫马大为.
Wǒ xìng Mǎ, jiào Mǎ Dàwéi.
My surname is Ma, called Ma Dawei.
林娜 你是加拿大人吗?
Nǐ shì jiānádà rén ma?
Are you Canadian?
马大为 我不是加拿大人, 我是美国人, 也是语言学院的学生. 我学习汉语.
Wǒ bùshì jiānádà rén, wǒ shì měiguó rén, yěshì yǔyán xuéyuàn de xuéshēng. Wǒ xuéxí hànyǔ.
I am not a Canadian, I am an American, and a student of a Language Institution. I learn Chinese.
