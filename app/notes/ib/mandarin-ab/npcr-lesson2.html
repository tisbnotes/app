1. How are your parents?

力波 陆雨平, 你好吗?
Lù Yǔpíng, nǐ hǎo ma?
Lu Yuping, how are you?
陆雨平 我很好. 你爸爸，妈妈好吗？
Wǒ hěn hǎo. Nǐ bàba, māmā hǎo ma?
I am good. How are your mom and dad?
力波 他们都很好. 你忙吗？
Tāmen dōu hěn hǎo. Nǐ máng ma?
They are all good. Are you busy?
陆雨平 我不忙. 你男朋友呢？
Wǒ bù máng. Nǐ nán péngyǒu ne?
I am not busy. What about your boyfriend?
力波 他很忙.
Tā hěn máng.
He is very busy.

2. Do you want coffee?

丁力波 哥哥，你要咖啡吗？
Gēgē, nǐ yào kāfēi ma?
Brother, do you want coffee?
哥哥 我要咖啡.
Wǒ yào kāfēi.
I want coffee.
弟弟 我也要咖啡.
Wǒ yě yào kāfēi.
I want coffee too.
丁力波 好，我们都喝咖啡.
Hǎo, wǒmen dōu hē kāfēi.
Good, Let’s all have coffee.
