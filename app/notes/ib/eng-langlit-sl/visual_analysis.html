Visual Analysis

  • Signifier: the graphic/written representation of something
  • Signified: the concept the representation may stand for
  • Icon: the (often) pictorial representation of something. It resembles
    what it represents
  • Symbol: a symbol is a representation of an idea, a concept or a value
    any object or person may stand for
  • Index: causal relationship between signifier and signified, then
    image is called indexical
    • In an indexical relationship the audience imagines what has happened
      or what will happen
    • Example: an image of smoke means there has been a fire; a bruised eye means a fist fight
