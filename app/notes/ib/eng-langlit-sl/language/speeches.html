Speeches

Types of speeches

  1. Oration- a pompous, ceremonial or rhetorical address on a momentous occasion.
  2. Sermon/Homily- a speech delivered with some moral advice.
  3. Exhortation- designed to encourage, inspire or stimulate to action-in war.
  4. Address- a formal speech to an audience.
  5. Panegyric- an oration of celebration or praise.
  6. Eulogy- a funeral speech in tribute to the dead.
  7. Valedictory- a farewell address generally at a graduation ceremony.
  8. Lecture- an informative address to instruct or expose a topic.
